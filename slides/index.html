router/dealer

develop a multiplayer game lobby

  • server is listening on 4 ports for entering the lobby
  • clients can connect to any port, they reach the same lobby
  • once in the lobby clients should receive a list of other connected clients
  • clients have a ready state that they can trigger
  • clients can chat in the lobby
  • once all clients in lobby are ready at the same time, clients join a 'game' chatroom and leave the lobby one
  • playing clients cannot chat in the lobby
  • new incoming clients can see the game chatroom but not talk in it
  • after 1 minute the game room closes and playing clients are returned to the lobby
